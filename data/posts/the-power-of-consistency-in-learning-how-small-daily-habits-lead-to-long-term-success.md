# The Power of Consistency in Learning

Learning something new can feel exciting at first, but many people give up after a short time. The secret to real progress is **consistency**, not perfection.

## Why Consistency Matters

When you learn a little every day, your brain has time to absorb and remember information. Small daily efforts add up faster than occasional long study sessions.

**Benefits of consistent learning:**
- Builds strong habits
- Reduces stress and burnout
- Improves long-term memory
- Keeps motivation steady
