Hi there

Selling domain names online can be a great business if you set it up the right way. Many beginners think a VPS alone is enough, but in reality, you need a few extra tools and services to get started.

## What Is a VPS?

A VPS (Virtual Private Server) is a server that allows you to host websites, applications, and services. It gives you full control compared to shared hosting and is commonly used for hosting business platforms.

## Is a VPS Enough?

A VPS is **not enough by itself** to sell domain names. You also need to connect with a domain registrar who has permission to register domains.
